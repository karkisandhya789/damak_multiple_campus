Welcome To Damak Multiple Campus

"Quality Assurance Accreditation (QAA) Certified Campus"

Damak Multiple Campus is a community-based campus located to the eastern region of Nepal. It is one of the leading community campuses in Nepal, situated in Jhapa district, Damak municipality ward no. 09, about 200 meters south to East-west lok highway. Initially DMC was basically the educational centre for the local people. However, at present , its catchment area is extended to the eastern part of Jhapa, Ilam, Panchthar, Taplejung , Terthum , Bhojpur, Dhankuta and neighboring VDCs of Morang district.

Our Words

NETRA BUDHATOKHI
Campus Chief

It is our pleasure to welcome all the students, parents, guardians and stakeholders at Damak Multiple Campus. This campus has a glorious milestone of three decades on delivering quality education. It has reputed, qualified and experienced faculties. Many faculties have experience of more than decades. Their performance is innovative, technologically equipped and community recognized. They always try to encourage and motivate students to achieve their goals. We focus on the students to reflect their dimensions through oral and written demonstration as well as extra-curricular activities. We want to open innumerable opportunities and scopes through diverse subjects and programmes. DMC, unparalleled, distinguished and most responsible campus in the educations arena of Eastern Nepal, has been pacing ahead to meet the aspirations of the society and national needs. It has majority of the students who are extraordinarily excellent, marginalized, dalits and the girls. Hence DMC is awarded QAA certificate by the UGC Nepal; the only certified campus in the eastern Nepal. We have enough infra-structures, library books, science labs for effective teaching and learning.
